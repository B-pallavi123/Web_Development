RGUKT

"RGUKT RK Valley" (Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, RK Valley) is a premier autonomous institution in Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established to provide quality technical education to rural and meritorious students. Located in "Idupulapaya, Kadapa district, the campus offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. The university follows a unique "six-year integrated B.Tech program", focusing on a strong foundation in science and mathematics. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, research opportunities, and a skill-based curriculum, RGUKT RK Valley plays a vital role in empowering students with knowledge and innovation..
